Member payment (MPesa)

Follow these instructions to pay via MPesa:

  1. Open your M-Pesa on your phone
  2. Choose Lipa na M-Pesa → Paybill
  3. Business No: 123456 (replace with your Paybill), Account: MemberNo or VYG
  4. Enter amount and pay — or click the button below to initiate STK Push (server required).
